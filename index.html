    About the Bootcamp

    This Startup Bootcamp facilitates aspiring entrepreneurs in acquiring the skills to assess and select viable startup ideas, navigate the journey from idea to company formation, and enhance the likelihood of establishing a thriving and successful startup.

    Bootcamp Goal

    Comprehensive bootcamp covering startup idea assessment, MVP development, monetization strategies, finance fundamentals, legal aspects, and more.

    Course Format

    Experience a well-structured course with engaging modules and purposeful assignments, creating a seamless learning journey tailored to your success.

    Open to All

    This course welcomes and embraces students from NIT Jamshedpur and individuals from diverse institutions, ensuring inclusivity and accessibility to a broader audience.
